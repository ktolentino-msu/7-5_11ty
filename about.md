# About the Possible 

This blog started because I had too many thoughts and nowhere to put them. Late at night, when everything’s quiet and the world feels bigger than it’s supposed to, I’d write little notes to myself just to make sense of it all. Sometimes it felt cosmic, sometimes it was just overthinking — but either way, I needed a place to land those thoughts.

Over time, those random notes turned into this. A kind of running log of things I’m noticing, questioning, or trying to understand. It’s not polished or perfect — it’s just me, looking up at the vastness of everything and trying to say something about it. If you’ve ever felt small, curious, untethered, or quietly in awe, you’ll probably feel at home here.
